About Think Stocks

Our team focuses on helping you communicate with others

#1 for Communication

Our team members provide the best user experience for anyone wanting to chat about stocks. Chat with confidence and share your personal insight

Learn Quickly how to Trade

Think Stocks not only helps its users share their own ideas but they also help users learn how to trade. Become familiarized with Wall Street vocabulary. Understand key differenced from a Bullish Market to a Bearish one.

Chat surveillance

Our team makes sure we have real users chating and we make it hard for fake accounts to be created. This surveillance process not only helps the existing users but it also helps new users not be guided in the wrong direction.

Events based on Markets

Our events page will notify you when there are events in your nearby area. Don't miss these important events.

Events based on Users

Our events page will also notify you when a user with a huge following will host an event as well. These events help new users adapt to trading and sharing stock information. Our team feels like the best learning opportunity is from users that learned through our system.

Real Time News

Think Stocks has a unique deal with news company, get notified for real time news. The user will be able to go directly to the website that provided the news.
